Spicy Egg Noodles

Description

Spicy noods

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of egg noodles
  • 1 tbsp of minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp of chilli oil
  • 1/2 tbsp of rice vinegar
  • 1/2 tbsp of brown sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp of soy sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water
  2. Mix garlic, chilli oil, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and soy sauce in small bowl
  3. Boil egg noodles until they have an al dente texture
  4. Drain the noodles and add sauce mixture to the pot
  5. Serve!
