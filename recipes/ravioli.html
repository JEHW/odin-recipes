Ravioli Recipe

Description

Italian done easy.

Ingredients

  • 32 frozen raviolis
  • 1/2 can of tomato paste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp italian herb seasoning
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Start to boil a large pot of water
  2. While the water starts to boil, add 1 tbsp of olive oil to a medium pan
  3. Add 1 tbsp of minced garlic and cook garlic
  4. Add tomato paste, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper to pan
  5. Add ravioli to boiling pot and cook until al dente
  6. Add heavy 1 cup of cream to pan
  7. Add pasta to pan, mix, and serve
