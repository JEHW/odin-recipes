Salmon Patties

Description

Fish bread is what we call this.

Ingredients

  • 4 containers of salmon
  • 2 tbsp potato flakes
  • 1/2 cup minced onion

Steps

  1. Mix together salmon, potato flakes, and onion in a large mixing bowl
  2. form mixture into patties
  3. Bake at 350°F for 20 min
  4. Serve them patties
