Baked Tilapia

Description

A dish to make all of your broke pescetarian dreams come true.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen tilapia filet
  • 2 tbsp seasoning (your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/3 can green beans
  • 1/3 box macaroni & cheese
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Start thawing fish 30 min before cooking
  2. Pre-heat oven to 350°F
  3. Start to boil a medium pot of water
  4. Season thawed fish with olive oil and seasoning
  5. Bake fish for 13 minutes
  6. Pour macaroni noodles into boiling water and leave on rolling boil for 7 minutes
  7. Drain and pour green beans into microwave safe bowl
  8. Microwave green beans for 2 minutes
  9. Once macaroni is done boiling, drain the pasta
  10. Add cheese powder, milk, and butter to macaroni and stir until well mixed
  11. Remove tilapia from oven once 13 minutes is up
  12. Plate and serve!
