Spicy Tuna Sandwich

Description

Cheap way to annoy your coworkers.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of white chunk tuna
  • 1/4 cup of Kewpie mayo
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 2 slices of multigrain bread

Steps

  1. Remove tuna from can
  2. Mix tuna with 1/4 cup Kewpie mayo
  3. Stir in 1 tbsp of Sriracha
  4. Put tuna mix between 2 pieces of multigrain bread
